# Travel Guide — Banyuwangi & Kokoon Hotel

> Practical travel information compiled from guest reviews and local knowledge.

## Getting to Banyuwangi

### By Air
- **Blimbingsari Airport (BWX)** — 15 minutes from Kokoon Hotel
- Direct flights from Surabaya (1 hour), Jakarta (2 hours), and Denpasar/Bali
- Taxi/Grab/Gojek available at airport

### By Ferry (from Bali)
- **Ketapang Port** — 15 minutes from Kokoon Hotel
- Regular ferries from Gilimanuk, Bali (30-60 min crossing)
- Ferries run 24/7, depart every 30-60 minutes
- Car and motorbike transport available

### By Train
- **Banyuwangi Baru Station** (~20 min from hotel) — trains from Surabaya, Yogyakarta, Jakarta
- **Banyuwangi City Station (Karangasem)** (~15 min) — local trains

### By Bus
- **Banyuwangi Terminal** (~15 min from hotel)
- Bus from Surabaya (8-10 hours), Malang, Jember, Denpasar

### By Car
- Surabaya to Banyuwangi: 5-6 hours via toll road + Trans Java Highway
- Denpasar to Banyuwangi: 3-4 hours including ferry crossing

## Best Time to Visit

- **Dry season (April–October)**: Best for Ijen trekking, Baluran safari, and outdoor activities
- **Wet season (November–March)**: Ijen can still be visited but trails are more slippery
- **Weekends**: Hotel gets busier, especially Friday-Saturday
- **School holidays**: Popular for families; book in advance

## Getting Around Banyuwangi

- **Ride-hailing**: Grab and Gojek available
- **Taxis**: Available at hotel and city areas
- **Rental**: Motorbike (IDR 50-100k/day) and car (IDR 300-500k/day) available through hotel
- **Private driver**: Can be arranged by hotel for day trips
- **Walking**: Hotel is not walking distance to city center — transport recommended

## Must-Do Activities

### 1. Ijen Crater Sunrise & Blue Fire
- Departure: ~12:00 AM – 1:00 AM from hotel
- Drive: ~1 hour from Kokoon Hotel
- Trek: 3 km, ~1.5-2 hours
- Best time: Dry season
- Hotel can arrange: Tour, early breakfast, transport, guide

### 2. Baluran National Park ("Little Africa")
- Distance: 1.5 hours drive from hotel
- Highlights: Savannah, wildlife, mangrove forest
- Best time: Early morning

### 3. Pulau Merah (Red Island)
- Distance: 45 min drive
- Activity: Surfing, swimming, beach relaxation
- Note: Accessible during low tide

### 4. De Djawatan Forest
- Distance: 30 min drive
- Highlights: Towering Albasia trees, magical forest atmosphere
- Popular for: Photography, Instagram

### 5. Kemiren Traditional Village
- Distance: 20 min drive
- Experience: Osing culture, traditional houses, local cuisine

### 6. Alas Purwo National Park
- Distance: 2 hours drive
- Highlights: Surfing at Plengkung (G-Land), wildlife, turtle conservation

### 7. Sukamade Turtle Beach
- Distance: 3 hours drive
- Activity: Night-time turtle nesting viewing (seasonal)

## Travel Tips from Guests

1. **Book Ijen tour through hotel** — Staff are experienced and provide early breakfast
2. **Request a high floor** — Better views and less noise
3. **Check breakfast timing** — Go early (before 8:30 AM) for best selection
4. **Bring earplugs** — If you're a light sleeper, due to mosque/road noise
5. **Confirm water charges** — Some guests noted drinking water is not complimentary
6. **EV users** — Free and paid charging available in basement
7. **Double-check room soundproofing** — Deluxe rooms may have better insulation
8. **English communication** — Basic English at front desk; use translation app if needed
